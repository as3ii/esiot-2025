### Acronyms
Drone Hangar = DH
Drone Remote Unit = DRU

## Notes
- Being the error states encoded as states in the FSM, no dedicated methods to
  send "ALARM" from DH to DRU is needed
- Temperature monitoring is always active, otherwise there would be no way to
  refuse take-offs and landings before they begin
- In the video, the L2 LED don't blink because the cable broke and I don't have
  any replacement
- Due to poor cables available in the kit, with moderately high resistance,
  the temperature measured is higher then expected and the LCD don't have
  the right contrast, and when the motor spin this get worse
